Legal Rights

"Awareness about Your Rights gives You the Might to Treated Right"

Do women have the right to get free legal aid?

In India, yes, women have the right to get free legal aid regardless of their financial situation. This is an important right that ensures equal access to justice for all women


Do women have the rights against being stalked ?

Women (and everyone!) have the right to feel safe and secure. This includes protection from stalking:
Unwanted contacts, fear and intimidation


Do female sexual assult victims have the rights to keep their identity anonymous ?

Yes, female sexual assault victims absolutely have the right to keep their identity anonymous in many countries
Privacy, security, encouragement to report, reduced stigma all the right are given.


Do women's have rights against workplace harassment ?

Yes, women (and all employees) have strong rights against workplace harassment. This means they have a legal right to work in an environment free from:
Unwanted sexual advances: This includes verbal harassment, groping, or any behavior that creates a hostile work environmen


The Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act (2005):

The Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act (2005) is a significant law in India that offers women crucial protection against domestic violence.
The Act safeguards women from violence by any adult male they are or were in a domestic relationship with, including husbands, partners, fathers-in-law, etc.Domestic violence goes beyond physical harm. It includes emotional, verbal, sexual, and economic abuse.


The Medical Termination of Pregnancy Act (MTP Act) of 1971 :

It is a landmark legislation in India that legalised abortion under specific conditions. Here's a concise explanation: Safe and Legal Termination: The Act allows for the termination of pregnancies by registered medical practitioners under certain circumstances. This helps reduce unsafe abortions that can be life-threatening. Protects Women's Rights: It recognizes a woman's right to make choices about her body and reproductive health.
