About Self Defense

Self-defense is about awareness and escape. Focus on vulnerable targets like the groin, eyes, or throat. Practice basic strikes like elbow strikes or using everyday items for defense. Most importantly, be loud and assertive to deter an attacker and create an opportunity to escape.

Self-Defense Techniques

Situational Awareness

Be aware of your surroundings and trust your gut instincts. Avoid poorly lit areas and walking alone at night. If you feeluncomfortable, remove yourself from the situation.

De-escalation Tactics

If possible, try to de-escalate the situation. Maintain eye contact, speak firmly, and set boundaries. If the person continues to threaten you, move away and call for help.

Basic Strikes

Learn basic self-defense strikes that target vulnerable areas. This could include elbow strikes, knee strikes, or palm heels. Consider taking a self-defense class to learn proper technique.

Using Everyday Items

If you're caught off guard, use everyday items for defense. Keys, pepper spray, or even a rolled-up magazine can be used to deter an attacker. Aim for the eyes or face and create an opportunity to escape.

Staying Calm

Staying calm under pressure is crucial. Take deep breaths and focus on your actions. Remember, your goal is to escape, not engagein a fight.
