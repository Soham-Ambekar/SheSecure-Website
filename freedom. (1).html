About Women Freedom Fighters

"Women freedom fighters have played a vital role in shaping history through their courage and resilience. From leading protests to fighting on the front lines of movements for independence and equality, these remarkable women have left an indelible mark on society."

Women Freedom Fighters

Rani Lakshmibai of Jhansi

A warrior queen who fiercely resisted British rule.

Sarojini Naidu

A poet, orator, and political leader who played a key role in the Indian National Congress.

Kasturba Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi's wife, a symbol of strength and resilience who actively participated in movements for freedom.

Begum Hazrat Mahal

The leader of Awadh who rebelled against the British after the annexation of her kingdom..

Aruna Asaf Ali

A freedom fighter who unfurled the Indian flag during the Quit India Movement.

Vijaya Lakshmi Pandit

A diplomat and politician who advocated for India on the international stage.
